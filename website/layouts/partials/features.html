Why Harbor Satellite

Extend your container registry to every edge location with reliability, security, and central management.

Reliability

Local registry at each edge location. Workloads always have access to the images they need, even when the network goes down.

Security

SPIFFE/SPIRE zero-trust identity with automatic credential rotation. No static secrets shipped to edge devices.

Fleet Management

Centrally manage image distribution across hundreds of edge locations. Group images and assign them to satellites.

Air-Gapped Ready

Works without internet. Images are served from the local Zot registry. Replication resumes automatically when connectivity returns.

CRI Integration

Auto-configure containerd, Docker, CRI-O, and Podman to use the local registry as a mirror with upstream fallback.

Lightweight

Single binary with an embedded Zot OCI registry. Minimal resource footprint for constrained edge environments.
